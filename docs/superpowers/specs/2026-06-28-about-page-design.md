# About Page Design

## Goal

Turn `/about` into an about page with a clean, premium, journey-led story about mangoes moving from farm to plate, while keeping a small practical CTA at the end.

## Direction

The page should feel editorial, restrained, and polished rather than loud or overly playful. The page should sell freshness and care through pacing, spacing, and copy.

## Structure

1. Hero
- Short label
- Strong headline about the journey from mango farm to plate
- One concise paragraph about ripe fruit, freshness, and balance with savory food

2. Journey section
- Three story cards
- Source: fruit chosen for ripeness and flavor
- Prep: fruit handled in small batches for drinks and kitchen prep
- Serve: mango meets burgers, drinks, and comfort food on the final plate

3. Freshness section
- Two-column editorial band
- Left side explains rhythm and ingredient care
- Right side gives a few short principles like ripe fruit, small-batch prep, and order-time finishing

4. Brand section
- Explain why Mango Factory is not only a drink shop
- Tie together mango drinks, desi burgers, and comfort dishes as one menu point of view

5. CTA footer
- Keep order, menu, and directions actions
- Smaller and calmer than the current utility-first page

## Styling

- Use existing visual language from the site
- Add more whitespace and calmer composition
- Avoid utility-card-first layout at the top
- Keep responsive layout simple

## Acceptance

- `/about` reads as an about page first
- Copy references journey, freshness, and ripe mangoes
- Layout feels clean and premium
- Practical actions still exist, but only at the end
